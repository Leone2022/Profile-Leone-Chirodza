About Me

Pastor Leone Chirodza is a Native Zimbabwean currently residing in South Africa. He is devoted young man who has committed his life to the ministry. Powerful preacher who seeks to prepare the saints for the coming of Jesus. He holds CCNA certificates in computer networking from Cisco academy; Bachlor of Theology and he is currently completing his Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering at Bugema University in Uganda. He is an invested master guide and an active member in the youth ministry. Pastor Leone loves nature and enjoys recreational activities.
